Smash Burger

The ultimate cheese burger recipe

Enjoy a crispy, juicy burger with a caramelized crust in minutes. This quick smash burger is cooked at high heat for a flavorful, charred patty. Serve on a soft bun with your favorite toppings for a delicious, satisfying bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and form into patties.
  2. Season the patties with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Place the patties in the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Top each patty with a slice of cheese and cook for an additional minute.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns in the skillet.
  7. Spread mayonnaise on the bottom bun.
  8. Place the patty on the bun and top with lettuce, tomato, and onion.
  9. Top with the other half of the bun and serve.
