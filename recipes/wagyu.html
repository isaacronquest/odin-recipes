Wagyu Steak

Waygu

Wagyu steak, famed for its rich marbling and buttery flavor, offers a melt-in-your-mouth experience. This recipe will guide you to perfectly seared wagyu, turning any meal into a moment of luxury.

Ingredients

  • 2 Wagyu steaks
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Remove the steaks from the refrigerator and let them come to room temperature.
  2. Season the steaks with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika.
  3. Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat.
  4. Add the olive oil to the skillet.
  5. Place the steaks in the skillet and sear for 3-4 minutes per side.
  6. Remove the steaks from the skillet and let them rest for 5 minutes.
  7. Slice the steaks against the grain and serve.
